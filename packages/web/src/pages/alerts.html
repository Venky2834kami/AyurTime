ℹ️ About Ayurvedic Dinacharya

Dinacharya means 'daily routine' in Sanskrit. Charaka Samhita (Sutrasthana Ch.5) prescribes a complete routine aligned with nature's rhythms. Following dinacharya balances the doshas, strengthens agni, and promotes longevity (ayu).
